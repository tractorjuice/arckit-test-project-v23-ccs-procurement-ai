# Crown Commercial Service (CCS) — AI Procurement Intelligence Platform

An architecture governance project using [ArcKit](https://github.com/tractorjuice/arc-kit) to design and govern the **AI Procurement Intelligence Platform** for **Crown Commercial Service**.

## Background

Crown Commercial Service (CCS) is an executive agency of the Cabinet Office responsible for improving government commercial and procurement activity. CCS manages over £30 billion in annual procurement spend across frameworks including G-Cloud, Digital Outcomes and Specialists (DOS), and Technology Services. With thousands of suppliers and millions of contract records, AI offers transformative potential for procurement intelligence.

## Project Scope

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Supplier Intelligence** | AI-powered supplier matching, capability scoring, and risk assessment |
| **Contract Analytics** | NLP analysis of contract terms, performance data, and compliance monitoring |
| **Spend Analytics** | ML-driven spend categorisation, anomaly detection, and savings identification |
| **Framework Optimisation** | Predictive analytics for framework utilisation and supplier pipeline |
| **Market Intelligence** | Automated market scanning for emerging suppliers and technology trends |
| **Fraud Detection** | AI models for procurement fraud indicators and bid-rigging patterns |
